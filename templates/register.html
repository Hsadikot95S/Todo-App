Frequently Asked Questions:

How do I add tasks with details?

Answer: To add a task, click on the "Add Task" button and fill in the necessary details such as task name, priority level, tags, creation date, and due date.

Can I edit existing tasks?

Answer: Yes, you can edit existing tasks by selecting the task and clicking on the "Edit" button. You can then modify the task details as needed.

How do I mark tasks as complete?

Answer: To mark a task as complete, simply check the box next to the task name in your task list. The task will then be marked as complete.

How can I receive reminders for tasks' due dates?

Answer: You can set reminders for tasks' due dates by selecting the task and choosing the option to set a reminder. You will receive a notification on the specified due date.

Is there an option to export tasks to CSV?

Answer: Yes, you can export completed tasks to CSV format by accessing the export feature in the app. This allows you to save and share your task data in a spreadsheet format.

What is the autocomplete feature?

Answer: The autocomplete feature suggests task names as you type, making it faster to add tasks to your to-do list. This feature is available to paid subscribers.

Can I schedule meetings within the app?

Answer: Yes, paid subscribers can schedule meetings via their favorite platforms such as Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and Zoom directly within the app.

How do I send reminders via email?

Answer: Paid subscribers have the option to receive task reminders via email. Simply enable the email reminder feature in your account settings to start receiving notifications.
